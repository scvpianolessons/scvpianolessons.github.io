About SCV Piano Lessons

Hi! I'm Andrew Martin, the founder and instructor of SCV Piano Lessons. I am a composer, producer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and educator.

I grew up in the Santa Clarita Valley. My neighbor down the street was a principal at a local elementary school. When I was sixteen, she asked me to give piano lessons to her daughters. I was initially reluctant; I had never taught anyone before! I realized immediately upon beginning to teach that it was something I loved to do. It is a wonderful feeling to be able to share my love of music with others and to inspire students to grow as musicians and fellow music lovers.

Since then, I've taught music professionally for over 8 years, from introductory preschool piano lessons to advanced music technology at UC Irvine and music theory at UC Berkeley, where I received my M.A. in Music Composition in 2016. I also studied at California State University, Northridge, where I recieved my B.M. in Music Composition in 2014. I have conservatory level training in classical, jazz and pop piano, and a wealth of performance and teaching experience.
